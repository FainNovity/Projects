  5. Every attack takes certain time unit to perform. This time unit is decreased from all waiting moves.
  6. Waitng moves are shown below in wwaiting move section
  7. Energy taken by attack-types :
    • -4
    • -2
    • 2
    • -5
  8. having insufficient energy won't let you perform attacks.
  9. Keep an eye on time unit so, that you know when your attacks come out of waiting stage.
